About

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate at Instituto Superior Técnico, University of Lisbon, focused on digital identity, self-sovereign identity, and decentralized trust frameworks. My work explores how to make identity systems technically robust, privacy-preserving, and socially trustworthy.

Research

Current research themes:

  • Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) and Verifiable Credentials.
  • Trust over IP (ToIP), trust registries, and governance frameworks.
  • Scalability and interoperability of digital identity infrastructure.

A curated list of publications, preprints, and talks will be added here soon.

Teaching & Supervision

I am interested in teaching topics at the intersection of distributed systems, cryptography, and digital identity, with an emphasis on connecting protocols to real-world governance and policy.
